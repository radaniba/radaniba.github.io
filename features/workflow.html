Success Story: Global Pharmaceutical Company

"MAESTRO Workflow Automation has transformed how we manage our clinical operations. We've reduced our study startup time by 62% while ensuring complete process compliance and visibility."

A global pharmaceutical company managing over 30 active clinical trials was struggling with inconsistent processes, delayed approvals, and limited visibility into process bottlenecks. Each therapeutic area had developed their own approach to managing study startup, document approvals, and site management—creating inefficiencies and compliance risks.

After implementing MAESTRO Workflow Automation, they established standardized processes for study startup, site initiation, monitoring visit management, and regulatory submissions. The visual workflow designer allowed clinical operations managers to create and modify processes without IT support, while maintaining compliance with company SOPs.

Within the first year, study startup time decreased from an average of 180 days to 68 days, document approval cycle times were reduced by 75%, and the team eliminated over 12,000 hours of manual administrative work. Most importantly, the platform provided complete visibility into process status, with dashboards highlighting bottlenecks and automatically escalating delayed tasks to ensure timely completion.
