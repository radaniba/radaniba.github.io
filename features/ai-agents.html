Success Story: Global CRO

"MAESTRO's AI Agents revolutionized our approach to clinical document processing. What once took days now happens in minutes, with better accuracy and consistency."

A global contract research organization (CRO) was struggling with the massive volume of clinical documents that needed to be processed, classified, and filed for their portfolio of 50+ active studies. Their team of 25 document specialists was overwhelmed, with backlogs growing and inspection-readiness suffering.

After implementing MAESTRO AI Agents, the CRO deployed document processing agents that automatically classified incoming documents, extracted key metadata, identified missing information, and routed documents to the appropriate reviewers. The conversational agents helped study teams quickly find information and answer regulatory questions.

Within three months, document processing time decreased by 82%, filing accuracy improved to 98%, and the team eliminated their backlog. Most importantly, they could reassign 15 document specialists to higher-value activities while maintaining complete inspection readiness across all studies.
