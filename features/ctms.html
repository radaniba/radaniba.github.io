Seamless Integration

MAESTRO CTMS integrates seamlessly with other MAESTRO modules to create a comprehensive clinical operations platform:

CTMS + eTMF

Trial activities automatically generate and file required documents in the appropriate TMF structure, ensuring complete documentation of trial activities.

CTMS + QMS

Quality events are linked to specific studies and sites, providing complete context for deviations, CAPAs, and audit findings with bidirectional tracking.

CTMS + Workflow

Study processes like site initiation, monitoring visits, and closeout activities follow standardized workflows with automated handoffs and approvals.

CTMS + AI Agents

AI-powered analytics provide predictive insights into enrollment trends, site performance, and resource requirements to optimize trial execution.

Success Story: Mid-Size Biotech

"MAESTRO CTMS transformed our clinical operations. We've reduced our study startup time by 42% while gaining real-time visibility across our entire trial portfolio."

A mid-size biotech company was struggling to manage their expanding portfolio of seven Phase II and III oncology trials across 120 global sites. Their fragmented systems and manual processes resulted in delays, compliance issues, and limited visibility into trial performance.

After implementing MAESTRO CTMS, they established a unified platform for managing all trial activities, with automated workflows for site management, patient tracking, and financial processes. The real-time dashboards provided immediate visibility into enrollment progress, site performance, and budget utilization.

Within the first six months, they reduced study startup time from 9 months to 5.2 months, increased patient enrollment rates by 37%, and improved site compliance with fewer protocol deviations. The financial automation saved over 45 hours per month in payment processing time while ensuring accurate and timely payments to sites.
