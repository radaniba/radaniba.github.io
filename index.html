About MAESTRO

MAESTRO was developed by a team of life science researchers, AI specialists, and software engineers with firsthand experience of the challenges facing modern research organizations.

Our mission is to accelerate scientific discovery by eliminating administrative burdens and providing researchers with the tools they need to focus on innovation rather than paperwork.

We believe that the future of life science research will be increasingly driven by AI-powered tools that can handle routine tasks, identify patterns in complex data, and support human creativity and insight.
